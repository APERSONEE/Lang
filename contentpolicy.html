Content we want to see on Lang

Lang is meant to be a place where anyone can come and learn, regardless of who they are. We have worked to make our tools as accessible as possible, and part of that responsiblity rests on the shoulders of our Lang community. We expect everyone who uses Lang to follow these guidelines about content. Infractions can lead to removal of the content or termination of the account, depending on severity.

  • Be respectful, kind, and caring. We strictly forbid any discrimination on any basis, whether that be race, ethnicity, religion, gender, age, sexual orientation, disability, or anything else. Creating content that harasses or endangers another group of people is strictly forbidden and can result in termination of an account.
  • Stay on topic. Lang is not the place for your latest fanfic or blog post. Lang is not an image sharing website. Lang is not a chatroom.
  • Be wary of using copyrighted content. Respect the works of others, and posting content that infringens on intellectual property rights is strictly forbidden.
  • Respect people's privacy. Do not post any revealing private information, about yourself or others, on Lang.
  • Stay away from NSFW (Not Safe For Work) Content. Just don't post it.

With the cooperation and support of our users, Lang can be the learning community it was always meant to be.

Thank you for using Lang.
