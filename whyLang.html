A note from our founder.

Hi, I'm Nate, and I founded Lang in May of 2022 for one simple reason- because I wanted a studying experience better than existed at the time. As our about page says, I was studying for a French test and grew annoyed of the constant advertisements, or if not advertisements, the constant begging to "please turn off your ad-blocker." I felt like I had to spend much longer than necessary using these sites, or perform the tedious work of making physical flashcards. So, I decided to try my hand at making a studying program. I could continue forever about how Lang has grown and grown since that day in May, sometimes getting worse before getting better, but that's not what you're here for.

I want to tell you, potential Lang user, that we here at Lang truly believe in the power of accessible education. Locking these servies behind paywalls is not just bad because you have to watch ads, but because many are missing out on a better education. Sure, these sites don't replace a school, but they surely do help people perform better in school.

So, even if you do not decide to use Lang, I just want to say thank you for coming to our site and giving it a chance. Lang runs off of your support, as it is even more powerful than money.

Thanks for visiting,
